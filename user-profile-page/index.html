Connecting a fitness tracker

We use a service called Human API to connect with various different fitness tracking services such as Strava, Fitbit, Garmin etc. When you connect your device you are authorising Human API to access and store your fitness activity data so that they can provide it to us. 
 
You can choose to disconnect and have your data deleted by the Movember Foundation and Human API at any time. 
 
You can read more about how the Movember Foundation and Human API handle your data in our Privacy Policy.
